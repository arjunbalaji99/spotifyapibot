How The Compiler is Calculated
  • The compiler takes in you and all your friends Spotify listening data (your top 50 artists and songs).
  • Then, it creates a points system, with your most listened to song getting 50 points, your 2nd most listened to song getting 49 points, and so on.
  • It then combines everyone in the groups songs and their point totals.
  • Then, for each user that has the song/artist in their top 50, it will multiply that song/artists point total by the number of users squared.
  • Finally, it displays the top 50 combined songs and artists for your enjoyment!
